Refund & Cancellation Policy

No Refund & No Cancellation Policy

TheVaishnav maintains a strict no refund and no cancellation policy for all software products and services purchased.

Exceptions

A refund will only be issued in the following circumstances:

  • If you have been charged but the software license was not allocated to you
  • If there was a duplicate charge for the same software license
  • If the product is officially discontinued by TheVaishnav before delivery

Refund Process

In case you qualify for a refund under the above exceptions:

  • Contact our support team immediately with your purchase details
  • Provide the transaction ID and proof of payment
  • The refund will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • The amount will be refunded to the original payment method used for the purchase

Important Notes

  • All sales are final once the software license is allocated
  • Software activation or download constitutes acceptance of the product
  • Technical issues will be addressed through our support system rather than refunds
  • Refund requests for any other reasons will not be entertained
